Being a designer always seemed like the perfect job. Making things pretty, working peacefully in front of my computer – I coundn’t imagine anything better when I was 13. Then I got accepted into the communication design studies and learned that there’s more to design than that. I learned about the importance of design and which impact it can have (maybe this is something I already guessed).


I am glad to become a designer in this time, when the role of the designer is changing from only being the aesthetic applicator to becoming the problem solver. That’s why I fell in love with UX Design. You can contribute to a solution, may it only be to make the life of some people just a little bit easier, by creating something which is not painful to use.
